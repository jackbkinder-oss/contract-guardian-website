FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Contract Guardian.

How does Contract Guardian detect risky clauses?
Contract Guardian sends your contract text to Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash AI model with a detailed legal analysis prompt. The AI reads the full document and flags clauses that put the service provider — you — at financial or legal risk. Each flag is classified into one of 8 risk categories and assigned a severity level of HIGH or MED based on how much exposure you face.
What file formats are supported?
Contract Guardian supports PDF uploads and plain text input. When you upload a PDF, the app extracts and renders the full document with clause highlighting directly in the viewer. You can also paste contract text if you receive it as a Word document or email — just copy and paste into the text input. Contracts up to 80,000 characters are fully supported.
How does the AI analysis work?
Contract Guardian scans your contract and flags potentially risky clauses across 8 risk categories. Each flagged clause includes a plain-English explanation and a risk score. Pro users (Gemini 2.5 Pro) get up to 10 analyses per month with full risk breakdowns. Premium users (Gemini 3 Pro) get up to 20 analyses per month, AI chat for follow-up questions, and AI-suggested revisions written as actual contract language you could propose to your client.
Is my contract data kept private?
Contract Guardian runs entirely on your local machine. Your contract files and analysis results are stored locally in your app's data directory — never uploaded to our servers. The only external communication is the contract text sent to Google's Gemini API for AI analysis, which is governed by Google's API privacy policy. You can use your own Gemini API key for full control.
What are the 8 risk categories?
Contract Guardian checks for: Scope Creep (unlimited revisions or vague deliverables), Cashflow Risk (Net 60+ payment, clawbacks, retroactive pricing), IP Ownership (loss of background or pre-existing IP), Non-Compete (restrictions on working in your industry), Termination (cancellation without payment for completed work), Liability Trap (uncapped exposure or one-sided indemnification), Auto-Renewal Trap (silent renewal or steep exit penalties), and Exclusivity (forced exclusive dealing or MFN pricing).
What's the difference between Pro and Premium?
Pro gives you up to 10 contract analyses per month powered by Gemini 2.5 Pro — you get full risk scoring, flagged clauses, and plain-English explanations. Premium upgrades you to Gemini 3 Pro with up to 20 analyses per month, AI chat for asking follow-up questions about specific clauses, and AI-suggested revisions for every flagged clause — written as ready-to-use contract language.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, absolutely. There are no lock-in contracts. You can cancel your Pro or Premium subscription at any time from your account settings and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period. Your account will then revert to the Free plan. Your local analysis history is always yours — it stays on your machine regardless of plan.
